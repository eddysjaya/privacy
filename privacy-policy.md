# Privacy Policy for Jagra Patrol

_Last updated: December 5, 2025_

Jagra Patrol (“we”, “our”, or “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our application and services.

## Information We Collect

- **Personal Data:** We may collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, and other data you voluntarily provide.
- **Device Data:** We may collect information about your device, including device ID, operating system type/version, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information.
- **Usage Data:** We may collect usage data such as logs, analytics data, and app interaction information.
- **Location Data:** With your consent, we may collect precise or approximate location data for features such as patrol tracking.

## How We Use Your Information

- To provide and improve our services
- To personalize your user experience
- To communicate with you (e.g., notifications, service updates)
- To maintain app security and integrity
- For analytics and performance monitoring

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:
- **Service Providers:** Trusted third parties who assist in operating our app and services under confidentiality agreements.
- **Legal Obligations:** When required by law, regulation, or legal process.
- **Business Transfers:** In case of a merger, acquisition, or asset sale.

## Data Security

We implement security safeguards designed to protect your information. Please be aware that no security system is impenetrable. We encourage you to protect your own data and access credentials.

## Children's Privacy

Jagra Patrol does not knowingly collect or solicit data from children under the age of 13. If you believe we have collected such data, please contact us to request deletion.

## Your Choices

- You may manage permissions (such as location access) in your device settings.
- You may contact us to access, correct, or delete your data.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Policy to reflect changes in our practices. Updates will be posted in the app and on our website.
